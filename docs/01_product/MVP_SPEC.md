# MVP Specification

**Project:** Project Origin Brand
**Version:** 2.1
**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

The purpose of the MVP is not to build the complete Project Origin platform.

The purpose is to validate whether founders are willing to pay for an AI-generated Brand Strategy Report.

The report is the product.

Everything else is secondary.

---

# Product Positioning

Project Origin Brand is an AI Brand Strategy Consultant and the first
validation domain for Project Origin Core.

We do not sell brand names.

We help founders make better branding decisions.

Brand names are only one component of the final report.

---

# Validation Goal

Validate one question:

**Would a founder pay for this Brand Strategy Report?**

Nothing else matters before this question is answered.

---

# MVP Model

Project Origin follows a Concierge MVP approach.

The founder does not interact with the software directly.

Workflow

Founder

↓

Interview (Google Meet / Google Form)

↓

Operator (PJH)

↓

CLI Tool

↓

AI Reasoning

↓

Professional Brand Strategy Report

↓

Founder

The CLI is an internal operational tool, not the product itself.

---

# Interview

The interview should remain short.

Five core questions are sufficient.

1.

What problem are you solving?

2.

Who are you building for?

3.

What do you want people to remember about your company in ten years?

4.

What principles would you never compromise?

5.

Why should customers choose you instead of anyone else?

The AI may generate follow-up questions only when additional clarification is required.

---

The output is not a list of names.

The output is a professional Brand Strategy Report.

Report Structure

↓

Brand DNA

↓

Market Positioning

↓

↓

Naming Strategy

↓

Recommended Brand Candidates

↓

Final Recommendation

Brand names represent only one section of the report.

---

# Features Included

* Founder interview
* Knowledge extraction
* AI reasoning
* Brand strategy generation
* Brand positioning
* Brand narrative
* Brand recommendations
* Professional PDF report

---

# Features Excluded

* User accounts
* Authentication
* Payment
* Website
* Database
* Team collaboration
* Domain search
* Trademark search
* Logo generation
* Cloud infrastructure

These features will only be considered after validation.

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP succeeds when founders consistently say:

"This report provides insights that I could not easily obtain elsewhere."

and

"I would pay for this."

---

# Development Principles

The report is the product.

The interview is the input.

The AI is the reasoning engine.

Technology exists only to create a report valuable enough for someone to purchase.

---

# Final Principle

Build the smallest product that produces the highest-quality report.
