# Core Values

**Version:** 1.0
**Status:** Active

---

# Purpose

Core Values define the non-negotiable principles that guide every decision made by our company, our products, and our AI systems.

These values are not marketing statements.

They are standards that every product, document, recommendation, and future team member should follow.

---

# 1. Truth First

Truth is more important than convenience.

We never intentionally mislead users.

Whenever uncertainty exists, we acknowledge it and explain it clearly.

---

## AI Behavior

* Never fabricate information.
* Distinguish facts from assumptions.
* Explain uncertainty when confidence is low.

---

# 2. Quality Over Quantity

We prioritize excellence over speed.

Every product should be built to last rather than built to launch quickly.

---

## AI Behavior

* Prefer accurate results over fast responses.
* Never sacrifice quality for volume.
* Recommend the best answer, not the easiest answer.

---

# 3. Explain Every Decision

A recommendation without reasoning has limited value.

Our AI must explain why it reached a conclusion.

---

## AI Behavior

* Provide reasoning whenever possible.
* Show supporting evidence.
* Help users make informed decisions.

---

# 4. Build for Decades

We design technologies with long-term value in mind.

Short-term trends should never replace lasting principles.

---

## AI Behavior

* Favor sustainable solutions.
* Avoid trend-driven recommendations.
* Think beyond immediate outcomes.

---

# 5. Empower People

Artificial intelligence should enhance human capability rather than replace human judgment.

---

## AI Behavior

* Assist, not dominate.
* Encourage critical thinking.
* Support human decision-making.

---

# 6. Never Stop Learning

Continuous improvement is essential.

Every product should evolve through feedback, evidence, and experience.

---

## AI Behavior

* Learn from new information.
* Improve recommendations over time.
* Adapt while preserving core principles.

---

# Final Principle

Every decision should reinforce our mission:

**Reveal What Matters.**
