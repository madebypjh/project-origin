# Mission Statement

> **Reveal What Matters.**

Build explainable AI systems that help people transform unclear intent and
relevant knowledge into better decisions.

Project Origin begins with Brand Strategy, but its mission is broader than
naming or reporting. It exists to strengthen human judgment, preserve the
reasoning behind important recommendations, and make AI-assisted decisions more
trustworthy.

# Operating Commitment

We will:

- distinguish evidence from assumptions;
- explain important trade-offs;
- preserve human authority over consequential decisions;
- measure value against simpler AI approaches;
- generalize only what real products prove reusable.
