# Research Pipeline

Version: Alpha 0.2

---

## Objective

Project Origin should continuously improve its Brand Intelligence Engine.

Rather than relying only on LLM creativity,
Project Origin studies successful brands
and extracts reusable brand patterns.

---

## Research Flow

Brand List

↓

Brand Analyzer

↓

Brand Genome JSON

↓

Pattern Extraction

↓

Pattern Database

↓

Naming Engine Evolution

---

## Research Components

### Brand Analyzer

Analyzes one or more famous brands.

Produces structured Brand Genome JSON.

---

### Pattern Extractor

Finds recurring phonetic,
semantic,
morphological
patterns.

---

### Genome Builder

Combines all analyzed brands
into a searchable Brand Genome Database.

---

## Long-term Goal

Build one of the world's largest structured Brand Genome datasets.

This dataset becomes
Project Origin's competitive advantage.

---

## Guiding Principle

Project Origin should learn from brands.

Not imitate them.
